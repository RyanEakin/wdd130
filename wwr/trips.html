we would love to accept your company in these grand adventures!

In Captain Cavera's Canal, ye landlubbers will be braving the final resting place of Captain Cavera. a legendary pirate captain who stalked the seven seas before meeting an untimely end within these very rapids! are you brave enough to conquer these treacherous flows of the canal? or are ye never gonna develop sea legs?

in night shoals rapids, the very rapids cover the balckest of stones cursed with dark magic and evil spirits! these very stones cause the phenominon that allows these rapids their force and power. though it be great family fun for everyone traversing these waters ever since we wrangled these spirits to our cause!

in Witches Cauldron rapids we see what happens when a witch forgets her boiling cauldron and it falls into the rapids! the alchemical mixture makes the rapids glow a faded blue for the potion multiplied within these waters. causing all sorts of magical effects for those who dare to enter the rapids... though be wary, for if you enter without aid... you will never be seen again!
