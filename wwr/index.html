Our Purpose: At Black Shoal Rafting Co. we endeavour to provide a family friendly spooky rafting experience. Our primary goal is to inspire people to brave the unknown and face it with wild courage. we believe that this not only benefits the individual, but the community by allowing people to take risks and have fun doing so! this also promotes a respect for mother nature unlike a textbook ever could.

  • Diverse Trip Options: Whether you're a beginner looking for a half-day family trip or an advanced paddler seeking a thrilling class V adventure, we have a variety of options to suit your experience level and interests.
  • Camping Adventures: For those looking to immerse themselves deeper into nature, our overnight and multi-day camping trips combine rafting with the tranquility of sleeping under the stars.
  • Guided Training: Interested in becoming a guide yourself? We offer comprehensive training programs for aspiring river guides, covering everything from safety protocols to rafting techniques.
  • Family-Friendly: Our family-friendly trips are designed to be safe and enjoyable for participants of all ages, with age-appropriate activities and equipment.
  • Guided Tours: Our experienced guides ensure safety and provide insights into the local environment, making each trip informative and engaging. They are trained in first aid and swift water rescue to guarantee a secure adventure.
  • Group Discounts: We offer special rates for groups, making it easier for friends, families, and corporate teams to bond over an exciting experience. Larger groups also benefit from personalized itineraries.
